Desired Behavior: The worm should efficiently navigate its environment to locate and consume food.

Reward Structure:
Positive Reward: +10 for consuming food.
Negative Reward: -1 for each time step to encourage efficiency.

Potential Shaping Rewards:
+1 for moving closer to food.
-2 for bumping into obstacles.


Performance Metrics:

Success Rate: Percentage of episodes where the worm finds and consumes food.
Average Steps to Food: The average number of time steps it takes the worm to find food.
Exploration Rate: Percentage of time the worm takes actions that haven't been frequently chosen before.
